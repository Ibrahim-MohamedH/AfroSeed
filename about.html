Who are we?

Afroseed is a fast-growing language service provider based in Africa. We contribute to the language industry by making most of its technology open source and accessible to everyone, that’s why we work to create a future where everyone can communicate more meaningfully and be understood. Ultimately, our daily commitment is about helping human potential thrive and encouraging diversity in all its forms.

Our Vision

Afroseed seeks to help improve worldwide communication through accurate, localized translations, interpretation services, and a wide variety of tailored language solutions for individuals, organizations, and businesses of all sizes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make communication easier and help organizations in many fields of expertise by finding better ways to connect with their audiences - anywhere in the world. We promise high-quality, accurate and affordable translations delivered within the set deadline. Also, we promise faster turnaround rates, multi-format support and enhanced user experience, in addition to 24 hours assistance and prompt delivery.

MS

Marketing Specialists
A) Main responsibilities of a marketing specialist:
  • Develop plans for marketing localization strategy.
  • Conduct market analysis for the locale.
  • Determining which channels to focus on.
  • Excellent organizational & communication skills.
  • Creating marketing guidelines for content creators.

D

Designers
A) Main responsibilities of a designer:
  • Maintain and adjust the design of the localized content or product.
  • If needed, create new designs to better attract local customers.

CW

Content Writers
A) Main responsibilities of a content writer:
  • Create new and original content in the target language.
  • Ideally filling some kind of content gap that can help you attract more customers in the new market.
  • Help enrich the existing materials your company has to share with the new target audience.

SD

Software Developers
A) Main responsibilities of a software developer:
  • Develop and adjust the software to local preferences.
  • Extract translatable and reintegrate translatable content to and from the software code.
  • Ensuring the localized product is bug free and runs correctly.

T/LE

Translators / Language Experts

Translators and language experts and the major “fieldworkers” of localization. They translate the content that needs to be translated, making sure it sounds natural and relatable to native speakers.

A) Main responsibilities of a translator:
  • Translate documents and marketing communications, paying close attention to the use of idioms, and slang.
  • Help with transcreation to maintain your brand voice and style.

LQA

Localization Quality Assurance (LQA) Specialists

Localization quality assurance is crucial for the success of your localization project, in which our experts review the localized product and ensure that it meets the target quality.

A) Main responsibilities of a LQA specialist
  • An LQA specialist will approach the localized product or content with a local perspective to identify any previously overlooked inaccuracies or inconsistencies
  • Test product or service and provide feedback for quality control.
